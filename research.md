# Research

My research focuses on the intersections of anthropology, cultural industries, and cultural policy in Asia.

Current research is organized into two major themes.

---

# Asian Content Industries

I study how cultural industries evolve through policy, technology, global markets, and industrial organization.

Research topics include:

- Games
- Film & Television
- Publishing
- Animation
- Cultural Technology
- Artificial Intelligence
- Cultural Policy
- International Content Markets

My anthropological research explores migration, food practices, and the social construction of "Southeast Asia" in Taiwan.

Research topics include:

- Migration
- Assemblage
- Cultural Economy
- Ethnography
