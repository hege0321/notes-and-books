# Selected Works

A selection of my publicly available research and industry analysis.

## Artificial Intelligence, Rights & Content Economies

### [出版業的內容資產化之戰（上）](https://research.taicca.tw/article/8381a11a-fba0-4da4-aa7e-73e4685ce0c2)

Publishing · Artificial Intelligence · Content Licensing · United States  
TAICCA Research, May 7, 2026

Examines how generative AI and zero-click search are disrupting publishing revenue models, and how publishers are shifting from traffic-based business models toward licensing and content rights management.

---

### [出版業的內容資產化之戰（下）](https://research.taicca.tw/article/1e83dd70-cbfc-40bf-916d-a95f48a7d0bc)

Publishing · Artificial Intelligence · Copyright · Content Economy  
TAICCA Research, May 7, 2026

Analyzes emerging AI licensing models in trade and academic publishing, with particular attention to collective bargaining, revenue sharing, and the structural disadvantages facing smaller publishers.

---

### [AI音樂不只是侵權爭議：授權談不攏、假播放湧現，分潤制度開始失效？](https://research.taicca.tw/article/9725d423-eeef-4c89-9322-d0ecf195874b)

Music · Artificial Intelligence · Streaming · Copyright  
TAICCA Research, April 16, 2026

Explores how AI-generated music is reshaping licensing negotiations, streaming fraud, royalty distribution, and the bargaining position of independent creators.
